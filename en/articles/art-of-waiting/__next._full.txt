<p>Patience is the most undervalued skill in trading. While the world celebrates action, the best investors understand that <strong>waiting is not passive</strong> — it is the most active form of discipline.</p>
<h2 id="the-market-rewards-patience">The Market Rewards Patience<a class="heading-anchor" href="#the-market-rewards-patience"><span class="anchor-symbol">#</span></a></h2>
<p>The market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient. This is not a metaphor — it is a mathematical reality. Every unnecessary trade costs you: commissions, slippage, and the opportunity cost of being wrong.</p>
<p>Jesse Livermore understood this better than anyone. He made and lost fortunes, but his greatest insights came from doing nothing:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>"It was never my thinking that made the big money for me. It was always my sitting."</p>
</blockquote>
<h2 id="the-rhythm-of-cycles">The Rhythm of Cycles<a class="heading-anchor" href="#the-rhythm-of-cycles"><span class="anchor-symbol">#</span></a></h2>
<p>Markets move in cycles. The key is not to predict them, but to recognize where you are in the cycle and act accordingly.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>"The four most dangerous words in investing are: 'it's different this time.'"
— Sir John Templeton</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The cycle repeats because human nature doesn't change. Fear and greed are constants. What changes is the narrative we wrap around them.</p>
<h2 id="when-to-wait-when-to-act">When to Wait, When to Act<a class="heading-anchor" href="#when-to-wait-when-to-act"><span class="anchor-symbol">#</span></a></h2>
<p>Waiting doesn't mean paralysis. It means:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Waiting for your pitch</strong> — Like Ted Williams' hitting zones, only swing at the fat pitches</li>
<li><strong>Waiting for confirmation</strong> — Let the market show its hand before committing capital</li>
<li><strong>Waiting for dislocation</strong> — The best opportunities come when others are forced to sell</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="conclusion">Conclusion<a class="heading-anchor" href="#conclusion"><span class="anchor-symbol">#</span></a></h2>
<p>Waiting is not doing nothing. It is doing the right thing at the right time.
